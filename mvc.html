About

This is a visual representation of an LTI system, or a 6.01 state machine.

  • We have created a simple oscillating demo. Click "Run" to see it in action.
  • Or, if you want to make your own, drag undesired components to the trash.
  • Click on a left panel button to create a new component.
  • Drag from a source (solid gray) to a target (empty blue) to create a connection.
  • Only an Adder is allowed multiple input connections!
  • Once everything is connected, run it or "Step" it for closer analysis.
  • Or click "See 1st 10" to quickly glimpse its long-term behavior/
  • The input defaults to Unit Sample. Click to toggle to Unit Step.
  • Right click on a component to reverse its orientation.
